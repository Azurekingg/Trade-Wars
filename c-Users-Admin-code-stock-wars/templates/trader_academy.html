Phase 1: Your Goal and Your Score

The Goal: Buy Low, Sell High

The only way to win a round is to have a higher Net Worth than your opponent at the final tick.

You increase your Net Worth by buying shares of stock when the price is low and selling them when the price is high.

Net Worth

This is your total wealth. It equals your current Capital (Cash) + the value of your Stock Held (Shares * Current Price).

PnL (Profit and Loss)

This is your score for the current round. It shows how much money you have made (Profit) or lost (Loss) compared to your starting capital of $100,000. The highest PnL wins the round!

Phase 2: Time and Price

What is a Tick?

A Tick is one unit of time in the match, like a second on a clock. Every tick, the stock price changes, and both you and the AI can execute one action. A match lasts for 300 Ticks.

Volatility

This is how quickly and drastically the stock price moves. High Volatility means the price swings wildly, offering high profit potential but also high risk. Abilities like 'The Pump' and 'The Dump' create massive, temporary volatility!
